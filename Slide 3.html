Who Is at Risk

Workers in physically demanding outdoor environments are particularly vulnerable to heat-related illnesses, including potentially fatal heat stroke

Construction Workers

Working with heavy equipment, physical labor, and often limited shade in hot environments

Mining & Industrial

Operating in confined spaces with poor air circulation and high ambient temperatures

Firefighters

Exposed to extreme heat conditions while wearing heavy protective gear

Outdoor Laborers

Landscapers, road crews, and agricultural workers with prolonged sun exposure
