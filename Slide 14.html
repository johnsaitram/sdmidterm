Cooling with PWM – Risk-based Control

Risk Level PWM Output Cooling State Description
< 0.3 Fan = 0%
Peltier = 0%		 Idle

Safe condition - no cooling needed
0.3 - 0.6 Fan = 40%
Peltier = 30%		 Pre-Cool

Warming up - preventive cooling begins
0.6 - 0.8 Fan = 80%
Peltier = 60%		 Full-Cool

Getting hot - strong cooling activated
> 0.8 Fan = 100%
Peltier = 100%		 Emergency

Overheat prevention - maximum cooling

PWM (Pulse-Width Modulation) enables precise control over cooling intensity rather than simple on/off switching, optimizing power usage and cooling effectiveness.
