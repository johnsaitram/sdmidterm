Detection Logic – Weighted Risk Index

We developed a weighted risk index approach where each sensor contributes to the total risk score:

• Each sensor outputs risk in [0.0, 1.0] range (0 = safe, 1 = critical)

• totalRisk = Σ(weight × risk)

Sensor Weight Why?
Core/Skin Temp 0.5 Most direct measure of overheating
Heart Rate 0.3 Rises with exertion or stress
GSR 0.2 Indicates sweat/stress response

This weighted approach enables proactive detection before the worker reaches a critical stage.
